Our approach

Our work starts with a fundamental question:

If your systems or supply chain are disrupted tomorrow, what is the minimum viable operation that must continue, and is your organisation prepared to protect it?

In critical environments, not every system carries equal weight. Not every process can be restored at the same speed. Not every decision can wait.

Resilience begins by defining what must continue to function, even under severe disruption. Defense Academy works with organisations to formalise that definition, understand your adversaries, train your employees and test in realistic conditions.

Services

  • Operational resilience assessments

    Structured engagements to define and validate minimum viable business or mission, map critical dependencies, establish recovery and escalation structures, and perform targeted gap assessments against ISO 27001, IEC 62443, NIST CSF 2.0, and EU NIS2 requirements, supporting preparation for Dutch implementation under the forthcoming Cyberbeveiligingswet.

  • Custom training programmes

    In-house programmes tailored to your operational model, including OT security for industrial environments, incident response in converged IT/OT systems, executive crisis decision making, and regulatory alignment.

  • Tabletop exercises & crisis simulations

    Scenario-driven exercises designed to test protection of minimum viable operation, decision quality under uncertainty, cross-functional coordination, external communication, and regulatory response.

  • Custom e-learning modules

    Scenario-based modules aligned to your environment and resilience priorities, used for onboarding, refresher cycles, and role-specific reinforcement.

  • Advisory & transformation

    Support in converting assessments and exercises into concrete changes in governance, architecture, and operating models, grounded in ISO 27001, IEC 62443, NIST CSF 2.0, CIS Controls, and threat-informed practices such as MITRE ATT&CK, ensuring resilience improvements are structural rather than temporary.
